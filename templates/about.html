ABOUT

Goal

The idea is to develop a web based portal which helps farmers of the country in multiple aspects. The basic features of the web portal are: A one time registration page for farmers. Enhanced readability with the use of pictures, visuals and multi - lingual support. Crop suggestions powered by big data analytics of historical crop production data obtained from authentic data sets taken from the Government of India’s Open government data platform (OCD), data.gov.in. Weather Pattern analysis using machine learning techniques such as Decision Trees( as data is non-linear) and time series forecasting. Information about possible pest attacks which are crop specific and solutions to tackle them. The source of solutions will be government funded national research organizations/ institutes. Details of nearby pesticide firms will also be available in the portal. Success estimator which will predict whether the crop will give a good amount of production or not based on local weather scenario, data science techniques and analytics of previous data. SMS Alerts in case of emergency events like cyclones based on MET department’s updates.
